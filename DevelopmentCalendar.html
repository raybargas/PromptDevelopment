How It Works

Focus Time

Every Tuesday through Thursday from 10am-5pm ET, developers join their pod's dedicated Discord channel. Developers step away from Teams and calendars are blocked. Pods work together with cameras on, enabling natural collaboration while maintaining deep focus. These consistent 7-hour blocks create predictable, protected time for feature development.

Blitz Days

Once monthly, we escalate to a single 8-hour hackathon-style development marathon. Complete isolation from meetings and messaging platforms allows pods to tackle complex features requiring sustained context. Breakfast and lunch are provided to keep the team fueled and focused. The Blitz Day concludes with a 2-3 minute video demonstration posted to Community, showcasing tangible progress and maintaining cross-team visibility.

Rev.io Thunderdome

Once quarterly, teams gather on-site for an intensive 4-day development sprint, tackling major modular projects. Working 12-14 hour days with transportation, meals, laundry, and cleaning provided, developers achieve deep collaboration that's impossible remotely. Each day concludes with a video demo showcasing progress. Cash prizes and bounties reward exceptional achievements and completed features. The Gauntlet transforms ambitious projects into delivered features through sustained, focused teamwork.
